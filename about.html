How To Play

Flip-in is a simple memory game where you need to find matching pairs of cards.
Each card has a picture on it and you need to turn over two cards to see if they match.
If they do, you can keep them flipped over. If they don't, the cards will turn back over.
The goal is to find all the matching pairs before time runs out.

To start the game, click on the "Start Game" button
